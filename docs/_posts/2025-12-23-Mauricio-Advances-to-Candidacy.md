On December 9th, third graduate student in the group has advanced to candidacy! Mauricio Bahena Garcia successfully passed his oral exam and can now focus all his efforts on his original scientific contribution that will be the bulk of his PhD thesis. Coincidentally, just after his oral defense, I accompanied him for a brief celebration of the [Chancellor's fellowship](https://ogs.ku.edu/chancellors-doctoral-fellowship) that he obtained earlier in the year. This is the most prestigious fellowship a graduate student at the University of Kansas can have. It covers 3 full years of stipend and tuition.
